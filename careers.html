Mid Level to Senior CGI Artist

We are always interested in hearing from talented 3D artists and 3D modellers with a degree in Interior Design or a similar discipline and proven computer and graphic skills who would like to grow up with 100CGI Studio.

The ideal candidate will also have:

  • Extensive knowledge and skills in 3D modelling and rendering software.
  • Extensive knowledge and skills in post-production software for still images and video.
  • Ability to work independently and within a team
  • Interest in architecture and photography
  • Constant desire for learning and dedication
  • High aptitude for design and multimedia skills
  • Attention to detail and ability to work well under tight deadlines.

Please send a link or an e-mail which maximum files size is 20 Mb (to do not saturate our server) with your CV and your portfolio to: career@100cgi.com
